STC Clubs

CMIT is a vibrant community of math enthusiasts at IISER TVM that promotes curiosity and creativity in mathematics. The club organizes a variety of activities such as math puzzles, problem-solving sessions, workshops, and celebrations like pi-week. CMIT also actively engages in outreach efforts to make mathematics more approachable and enjoyable for all students. Website:https://cmit.iisertvm.ac.in Instagram:@cmitiisertvm

The Chemical Society, a platform for chemistry enthusiasts to explore and appreciate the vastness of the chemical sciences. It hosts experimental demonstrations, lecture series, and chemistry-themed games and activities. CSIT encourages learning through fun and hands-on experiences. Instagram:@csit_iisertvm

PSIT (Physics Society of IISER TVM) PSIT is the Physics Society of IISER TVM. It organizes problem-solving sessions, seminars, physics outreach activities, and fun experimental demos. The society fosters curiosity and builds a strong community of students passionate about physics. Instagram:@psitiiser

Coding Club The Coding Club of IISER TVM fosters programming and software development skills. It organizes coding bootcamps, hackathons, and workshops on competitive programming, web development, and machine learning. The club is a platform to explore technology and build impactful projects. Instagram:@codingclubiisertvm

Proteus (Biology Club) Proteus is the biology club of IISER TVM, bringing together students with a passion for the life sciences. The club organizes workshops, science communication events, discussions, and invited talks from experts in biology. It aims to build a stronger biology community and foster collaboration among students and researchers. Instagram: @proteus.iisertvm

Ecological Society of IISER TVM (ESI) The Ecological Society is dedicated to promoting awareness and action on environmental and ecological issues. It conducts field visits, birdwatching trails, clean-up drives, sustainability workshops, and discussions on biodiversity and conservation. The society fosters a deep appreciation for nature and a strong sense of ecological responsibility. Instagram: @esi_iisertvm

QSI (Quizzing Society of IISER TVM) QSI is the official quiz club of IISER TVM. It organizes general and themed quiz competitions across science, literature, pop culture, and current affairs. The society encourages curiosity and broad-based knowledge while building a vibrant quizzing culture. Instagram: @qsi_iisertvm

Parsec (Astronomy Club) Parsec is the official astronomy club of IISER TVM. It provides a platform for students to explore the wonders of the cosmos through stargazing nights, telescope handling, astrophotography, and lectures on astrophysics and space science. Parsec also conducts outreach programs, making astronomy accessible to the larger community. Instagram: @parseciisertvm
